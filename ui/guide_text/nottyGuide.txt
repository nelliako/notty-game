NOTTY – How to Play

What the game is

Notty is a simple open-hand card game for 2–3 players.
All cards stay face-up, so everyone can see what everyone else has.

Cards come in 5 colors (red, green, yellow, black, blue) and are numbered 1–9.
Every color–number combo has two copies. The full deck has 90 cards.



Getting started


Shuffle the deck.

Deal 4 cards to each player.

Your hand is face-up the whole game.

The deck stays face-down.

Your turn

On your turn, you can do any of the actions below, in any order:

Draw up to 3 cards

Draw one card at a time, up to a maximum of three.
You don’t see what you drew until you stop drawing.

Take a random card from another player(Steal)

Pick someone and take one of their cards at random.

Draw 1 card and then discard 1(Trade)

Simple as it sounds.

Discard groups

You can discard as many valid groups as you want. A valid group is:

A run of 3 or more numbers in a row, all same color
(example: blue 4, blue 5, blue 6)

or

A set of 4 or more cards with the same number, all different colors
(example: red 4, blue 4, green 4, yellow 4)

Whenever cards are discarded, they go back into the deck and the deck gets reshuffled.

Pass

You can also do nothing.



Hand limit

You can never have more than 20 cards.
If an action would push you over 20, you can’t do it.



Winning

You win the moment your hand becomes empty.
This can happen because you discarded your last cards or someone stole your final card.
As soon as a hand hits zero, the game stops.
